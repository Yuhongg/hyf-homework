Ultra Facial Cream is Kiehl's iconic light-textured, up to 24-hour moisturiser that helps to retain moisture within the skin, leaving skin feeling comfortable and well-balanced.

Kiehl's unique, fast-absorbing cream provides water replenishment throughout the day by absorbing moisture from the air, for continuous comfort and moisture-balanced skin.
This "ultra hydrator" helps to strengthen the skin barrier and is formulated with oilve-derived Squalane, highly effective at helping to maintain skin's moisture. And Antarcticine, a Glycoprotein extracted from micro-organisms in sea glaciers, notable for it's ability to thrive in extreme climates.

- Reduces moisture loss
- Helps water replenishment
- Inspired by Kiehl's original, Ultra Facial Moisturizer
- Up to 24 hour ultra-hydrating moisturiser

Cool Kiehl's Fact: Through the Ultra Facial Cream, Kiehl's was honoured to lend support and protection from the elements to the heroic team of the "Greenland First Ascent" expedition.

Directions

Apply Kiehl's Ultra Facial Cream to clean facial skin day or night as needed to combat moisture depletion.
